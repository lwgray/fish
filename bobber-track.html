How this maps to the app

In index.html the fcWorker loop draws every camera frame with ctx.drawImage(value,0,0) (line 317) before encoding it into the pre-roll ring. The auto-zoom is the 9-argument form of that same call — ctx.drawImage(value, sx,sy,sw,sh, 0,0,W,H) — where sx,sy,sw,sh is the smoothed crop window this page computes. Detection runs on a downscaled copy (~160 px wide) so it costs ~1 ms/frame. When confidence drops below the gate the crop eases back to the full wide frame, so a lost track never ruins the shot.
